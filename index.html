Our vision

To augment the global calibration for Covid-19 related preventative solutions; specifically tailored to education, residential, recreational, hospitality and corporate sectors.

How does it work?

Our Electrostatic Spray Systems produce electrically charged spray droplets which are carried to the targets in a high-speed air stream.

Sanarc Disinfection Service

We combine our electrostatic sprayers with a highly effective sanitiser agent in order to combat Covid-19. 

Benefits of the service:

  • Extended kill of up to 14 days (on most surfaces)
  • Kills and neutralizes 99.99% of bacteria and viruses
  • Touchless disinfection
  • Reach complex areas with 360 degree wrap arounddisinfection layer Can be safely used on all surfaces including kitchenware, files, technology, etc.
  • Up to 8x faster than traditional sanitation services
  • Can be safely used on all surfaces and appliances including kitchenware, files, computers etc.
  • Environment safe for occupation minutes after application

How does our service differ?

Thanks to our patented sanitizer, our service offers a residual effectiveness of up to 14 days; when combined with ESS technology, our preventative package is not only far less time- consuming, but also, far more comprehensive in its preventative surface coverage. Our advanced techniques are guided by 25 years of industry-specific knowledge.

Recommended treatment procedure

Step 1: ESS spraying

Sanarc will do a full preventative treatment which encompasses a full coverage sanitation of the premises by means of ESS technology.

After completing the sanitation treatment, we provide a variety of safety and educational signs.

Step 2: Signage

Step 3: Sanitation stands

Hands-Free Sanitizing stands will be placed in areas which contain high human congregation, to ensure that hand sanitizer is readily available.

As a further means of precaution, ‘Ozone Technology’ can be placed in high- traffic areas, in order to decontaminate the surrounding air.

Step 4: Ozone

Step 5: External environmental swab

As a final measure to provide credible evidence that your environment is Covid- 19 free, one can opt for a swabbing test which we will complete, and send off to a laboratory for testing.
